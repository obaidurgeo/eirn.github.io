Our Work

Research & Projects

Impactful environmental research and consulting work across Bangladesh — from the Sundarbans to Sylhet, coastal zones to urban centers.

🌿
Coastal Ecology
Sundarbans Climate Resilience Assessment

Analyzing climate-induced changes on the world's largest mangrove ecosystem and its biodiversity using multi-source remote sensing data including Sentinel-1 SAR and Landsat OLI imagery.

🛰️ Remote Sensing 🗺️ GIS Mapping 📅 2023–2024
💧
Hydrological Modeling
Haor Wetland Flood Dynamics

Modeling seasonal flood extents in northeastern Bangladesh's haor basins to understand inundation patterns, their impact on livelihoods, and adaptation pathways for vulnerable communities.

🌊 Flood Modeling 📡 SAR Data 📅 2023
🏙️
Urban Environment
Dhaka Urban Heat Island Mapping

Spatiotemporal analysis of urban heat islands across Dhaka Metropolitan Area using Landsat thermal infrared data and machine learning classification to identify hotspots and cooling zones.

🌡️ Thermal Analysis 🤖 Machine Learning 📅 2022–2023
💨
Air Quality
Air Pollution & Health Risk Analysis

Exposure modeling of PM2.5 and NO₂ concentrations across urban and industrial zones in Bangladesh to quantify environmental health burdens on local populations.

💨 Air Modeling 🏥 Health Risk 📅 2023
🌀
Disaster Risk
Cyclone Vulnerability Mapping

Integrated GIS-based vulnerability and exposure assessment for coastal communities in the Bay of Bengal delta region to inform early warning and evacuation planning.

⚡ Risk Mapping 🗺️ GIS 📅 2022
🌾
Agriculture
Crop Stress & Yield Prediction

UAV and satellite-derived vegetation indices (NDVI, EVI) to monitor crop health and predict yield under climate stress conditions across major agricultural districts.

🚁 UAV Survey 🌿 NDVI Analysis 📅 2023–2024
🏞️
Water Resources
Groundwater Depletion in Barind Tract

Assessment of groundwater level trends and recharge potential in the drought-prone Barind Tract using GRACE satellite data and field hydrogeological surveys.

💧 Groundwater 📡 GRACE Data 📅 2022–2023
🌱
Agriculture
Soil Health Assessment — Char Lands

Spatial mapping of soil degradation and nutrient deficiency across riverine char lands using field sampling, laboratory analysis, and geostatistical interpolation techniques.

🌍 Soil Mapping 📊 Geostatistics 📅 2023
🐟
Coastal Ecology
Blue Carbon Stock Estimation

Estimation of carbon sequestration potential in tidal marshes and mangrove ecosystems along Bangladesh's coast to support REDD+ and carbon credit initiatives.

🌿 Carbon Stocks 🛰️ Remote Sensing 📅 2024
