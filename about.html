Who We Are

A multidisciplinary team of environmental scientists and geospatial experts committed to data-driven solutions for Bangladesh.

Our Mission

Science at the Service of the Environment

The Environmental Innovation and Research Network (EIRN) was founded with a singular purpose: to apply rigorous environmental science and cutting-edge geospatial technology toward solving Bangladesh's most pressing ecological and agricultural challenges.

We operate at the intersection of research and practice — conducting peer-reviewed studies while simultaneously delivering actionable consulting services to government bodies, NGOs, development partners and the private sector.

Bangladesh's unique geography — vast river delta systems, coastal mangroves, agricultural plains and rapidly urbanizing cities — demands innovative, locally-grounded environmental solutions. EIRN was built precisely to meet that demand.

Our Services

What We Do

Comprehensive environmental consulting and research, powered by advanced geospatial technology.

01
GIS & Spatial Analysis

Advanced mapping, spatial data analysis and cartographic outputs for environmental and land-use planning. EIRN delivers precise GIS-based solutions using ArcGIS, QGIS and spatial databases to inform critical environmental decisions.

ArcGISQGISSpatial DB
02
Remote Sensing & Satellite Analysis

Satellite imagery processing, land cover classification, vegetation monitoring and multi-temporal change detection. We process Sentinel, Landsat and MODIS data to generate actionable insights on Bangladesh's changing landscapes.

SentinelLandsatMODIS
03
UAV & Drone Surveys

High-resolution drone-based aerial surveys for agricultural monitoring, disaster assessment and coastal mapping. Our UAV operations generate centimeter-level photogrammetric outputs and detailed elevation models.

DJI DronesPhotogrammetryLiDAR
04
Environmental Modeling

Hydrological, flood risk, climate and ecosystem modeling to simulate scenarios and support evidence-based decisions. Using HEC-HMS, SWAT and MIKE FLOOD to produce reliable model outputs for Bangladesh's complex water systems.

HEC-HMSSWATMIKE FLOOD
05
Agricultural Research

Crop stress analysis, soil assessment, precision agriculture and climate-smart farming for rural Bangladesh. Multispectral UAV surveys and NDVI analyses help optimize agricultural productivity under climate stress.

Crop MonitoringSoil MappingNDVI
06
Policy & EIA Consulting

Environmental Impact Assessments, policy briefs and advisory services aligned with Bangladesh DoE regulations. Evidence-based policy recommendations for government ministries and international development organizations.

EIAPolicy BriefsDoE
Our Approach

How We Work

A structured, evidence-based methodology ensuring every project delivers real impact.

01
Scoping & Assessment

In-depth stakeholder consultations and site assessments to define the problem, scope and expected deliverables clearly.

02
Data Collection

Field surveys, UAV flights, satellite data acquisition and integration of existing datasets to build a comprehensive evidence base.

03
Analysis & Modeling

Rigorous geospatial analysis, statistical modeling and scenario simulation using industry-standard platforms and custom algorithms.

04
Reporting & Action

Clear, actionable reports with maps, visualizations and policy recommendations tailored for decision-makers.
