About EIRN

Science-Driven Solutions for Environmental Challenges

The Environmental Innovation and Research Network (EIRN) is a Bangladesh-based consultancy dedicated to solving complex environmental and agricultural problems through rigorous research, advanced geospatial technologies, and collaborative partnerships.

We work hand-in-hand with Government ministries, NGOs, and international organizations to deliver evidence-based insights that drive policy and on-the-ground impact.

Government & NGO collaboration on environmental policy
Cutting-edge GIS and remote sensing analysis
UAV-based field data collection and mapping
Agricultural resilience research for rural Bangladesh
"Bridging scientific excellence with practical environmental solutions for Bangladesh and beyond."

Our Services

What We Do

From field surveys to satellite analysis, we offer a full spectrum of environmental consulting and research services.

🗺️
GIS & Spatial Analysis

Advanced geographic information system mapping, spatial data analysis, and cartographic outputs for environmental and land-use planning.

🛰️
Remote Sensing

Satellite imagery processing, land cover classification, vegetation monitoring, and multi-temporal change detection across Bangladesh.

🚁
UAV & Drone Surveys

High-resolution drone-based aerial surveys for agricultural monitoring, disaster assessment, and coastal zone mapping.

🌊
Environmental Modeling

Hydrological, flood risk, climate, and ecosystem modeling to simulate future scenarios and inform resilient decision-making.

🌾
Agricultural Research

Crop stress analysis, soil assessment, precision agriculture, and climate-smart farming recommendations for rural communities.

📋
Policy & EIA Consulting

Environmental Impact Assessments, policy briefs, and government advisory services aligned with Bangladesh's environmental regulations.
