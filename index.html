Environmental Innovation & Research Network

Advancing Climate Intelligence, Geospatial Science & Environmental Systems

EIRN is a multidisciplinary research and consulting organization focused on wildfire risk, air quality, remote sensing, and environmental modeling to support resilient ecosystems and communities.

Research Divisions

Climate & Wildfire Systems

Wildfire risk modeling, HDW index analysis, and climate hazard mapping.

Air Quality & Health

Air pollution exposure modeling and environmental health risk analysis.

Remote Sensing & GeoAI

Satellite-based monitoring and machine learning for environmental systems.

Coastal & Hydrology

ROMS modeling, sediment transport, and coastal system dynamics.

Projects

Wildfire Risk & Health Accessibility

Assessing wildfire exposure and healthcare accessibility in Southern California.

Sundarbans Climate Resilience

Analyzing climate impacts on coastal ecosystems and biodiversity.

Understory Vegetation Modeling

Machine learning-based mapping of vegetation density using LiDAR and Landsat.
