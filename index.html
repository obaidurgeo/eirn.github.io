Who We Are

Message From Our Founder

EIRN stands out as a hub for rigorous environmental science and geospatial innovation in Bangladesh, offering cutting-edge solutions that integrate GIS, remote sensing, UAV technology, and open-source modeling platforms. These tools are pivotal in creating sustainable research designs and addressing complex ecological challenges across Bangladesh's diverse landscapes.

By applying systematic and science-driven thinking, EIRN transforms traditional environmental problems into opportunities for evidence-based policy and community resilience — particularly in large-scale hydrological modeling, coastal ecology research, and climate adaptation planning.

At the heart of EIRN lies a dynamic team of young, motivated professionals who embody the spirit of innovation and determination. Their energy and passion drive the organization's research-based approach to problem-solving, offering practical and implementable solutions to Bangladesh's most pressing ecological and agricultural challenges.

Our Expertise

Innovative Solutions for a Sustainable Future

A full spectrum of environmental consulting and research services, powered by advanced geospatial technology.

01
Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)

Environmental assessment is appropriate for both site-specific projects and broader programs. EIRN delivers comprehensive ESIAs that analyze, monitor, and manage the intended and unintended social and environmental consequences of planned interventions — ensuring projects align with Bangladesh DoE regulations and international best practices.

EIASocial ImpactDoE Compliance
02
GIS, Remote Sensing & Drone Technology

EIRN demonstrates exceptional expertise in GIS, remote sensing, and UAV technology, leveraging these advanced tools to revolutionize data collection, analysis, and visualization in environmental projects. High-resolution geospatial data essential for precise site analysis, sustainable urban planning, and infrastructure safety.

ArcGISQGISGoogle Earth EngineDJI DronesLiDAR
03
Hydrological Modeling & Flood Risk Analysis

Using HEC-HMS, SWAT, and MIKE FLOOD to model seasonal flood extents, simulate hydrological scenarios, and generate evidence-based flood risk assessments for Bangladesh's complex river delta systems and haor wetlands.

HEC-HMSSWATMIKE FLOODSAR Data
04
Agricultural Research & Precision Farming

Crop stress analysis, soil health assessment, precision agriculture and climate-smart farming research for rural Bangladesh. Using UAV-derived multispectral imagery and NDVI analysis to identify crop stress, predict yields, and develop evidence-based recommendations.

Crop MonitoringSoil MappingNDVIUAV Survey
05
Climate Change Adaptation Strategies

EIRN develops comprehensive adaptation strategies including infrastructure resilience planning, water resource optimization, climate-resilient agricultural practices, and ecosystem restoration — aligned with Bangladesh's national climate policies and international frameworks.

Climate ModelingRisk AssessmentPolicy Briefs
06
Policy Advisory & Government Consulting

Environmental Impact Assessments, policy briefs and advisory services aligned with Bangladesh DoE regulations. EIRN works hand-in-hand with government ministries, NGOs and international organizations to deliver evidence-based insights that drive policy and on-the-ground impact.

EIA ReportsPolicy BriefsGovernment Advisory
