FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of organizations does EIRN work with?
EIRN collaborates with government ministries, local and international NGOs, development banks, research universities, and private sector companies with environmental compliance needs.
Can EIRN assist with Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)?
Yes. EIRN provides full EIA services aligned with Bangladesh Department of Environment (DoE) guidelines and international standards such as IFC Performance Standards.
Does EIRN conduct field surveys and drone operations?
Absolutely. We have an in-house UAV team equipped to conduct aerial surveys, photogrammetric mapping, and multispectral image acquisition anywhere in Bangladesh.
How can I collaborate with EIRN on a research project?
We welcome academic and institutional research partnerships. Reach out through the contact form above or email us directly with your proposal or idea to start a discussion.
Does EIRN offer training in GIS and remote sensing?
Yes, EIRN periodically offers technical training workshops in GIS, remote sensing, and environmental modeling for professionals and students. Contact us to be notified of upcoming sessions.
What geographic areas does EIRN primarily operate in?
Our primary focus is Bangladesh, with particular expertise in coastal zones, river delta systems, haor regions, and major urban centers. We also support projects in neighboring South Asian countries.
