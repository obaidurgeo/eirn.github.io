FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of organizations does EIRN work with?+
EIRN collaborates with government ministries, local and international NGOs, development banks, research universities and private sector companies with environmental compliance needs.
Can EIRN assist with Environmental Impact Assessments?+
Yes. EIRN provides full EIA services aligned with Bangladesh Department of Environment (DoE) guidelines and international standards such as IFC Performance Standards.
Does EIRN conduct field surveys and drone operations?+
Absolutely. We have an in-house UAV team equipped to conduct aerial surveys, photogrammetric mapping and multispectral image acquisition anywhere in Bangladesh.
How can I collaborate on a research project?+
We welcome academic and institutional research partnerships. Reach out through the contact form above or email us directly with your proposal to start a discussion.
Does EIRN offer GIS and remote sensing training?+
Yes, EIRN periodically offers technical training workshops in GIS, remote sensing and environmental modeling for professionals and students. Contact us to be notified.
What geographic areas does EIRN operate in?+
Our primary focus is Bangladesh, with expertise in coastal zones, river delta systems, haor regions and urban centers. We also support projects in neighboring South Asian countries.
